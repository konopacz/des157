Leffen's Gauntlet

Help Leffen get to safety by avoiding enemies, just by talking!

Voice Commands

  • Say "Jump" to jump over things.
  • Say "Duck" to duck under things.
  • Say "Pause" to pause the game.
  • Say "Start" to start the game!

Good luck! Warning: voice commands take a second to register, so make sure you make your command early!
