StoryBoards

This first paper model showed me some of the problems with using voice as the primary navigation method. Because all web users are accustomed to clicking buttons and are trained to look for things that look clickable, an absence of these interface items may seem confusing. I also received the feedback that prompts for action or voice commands should appear in the same part of the screen every time.

This paper prototype allowed me to further explore my navigation system. This iteration, I received feedback concerning the timing of events and how long the user will have to make decisions. I also reflected more on the cycle of gameplayer, where my test user was confused by the way the game cycled back and forth.
